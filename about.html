About Poshak

35+ Years of Tailoring Excellence

Our Story

At Poshak, we believe that every stitch tells a story, and yours deserves to be told in style. With over 35 years of expert tailoring experience, we specialize in creating bespoke clothing that delivers a perfect fit and flawless style. Our passion for craftsmanship and attention to detail is woven into every piece we create.

  • Corporate Uniforms that elevate your brand's image
  • Event Apparel designed to make a statement
  • Bespoke Clothing tailored to your unique style

We bring the shop to you across Mumbai, ensuring convenience without compromising on quality. Discover the art of personalized tailoring with Poshak – where your style meets our craft.
